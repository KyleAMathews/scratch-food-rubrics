# Classified scratch-dessert corridor — exhaustive A/B/C inventory

Compiled 2026-07-16 exclusively from restaurant records already classified in this East Millcreek run. Scope: Millcreek/East Millcreek, Sugar House, 9th & 9th, 15th & 15th, the Avenues, and immediately adjacent central/east-SLC neighborhoods plausibly relevant to the user's drive area. No new restaurants were discovered or added.

**Hours convention:** “Yes” means open *after* 8 PM on at least one normal evening. A posted 8 PM closing is a **boundary**, not a reliable 8 PM destination. Identity, address, and operator hours were rechecked where needed on 2026-07-16; source-level evidence remains in the cited durable worker returns.
