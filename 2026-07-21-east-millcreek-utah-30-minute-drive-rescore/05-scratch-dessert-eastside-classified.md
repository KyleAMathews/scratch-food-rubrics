## Counts and concise shortlist

- **15 confirmed:** 10 dedicated/dessert-forward shops and 5 restaurants with meaningful scratch dessert programs.
- **6 plausible but unproven** dessert-production candidates.
- **1 explicit non-producer** retained to avoid a false positive.
- **Clearly open after 8 PM on at least one day:** Parfé Diem, Matcha Cafe Kyoto, Greenhouse Effect, Thirst, Hearth and Hill, Rawtopia, 'mina, and Salt & Olive — **8 places**. Picnic is an additional conditional Friday possibility; Hill's Kitchen and several others are 8 PM boundary cases.

Best compact late shortlist from the evidence: **Parfé Diem** for a dedicated dessert destination, **Matcha Cafe Kyoto** for fully in-house Japanese sweets, **Greenhouse Effect** for component-level scratch sweet crepes, **Thirst** for scratch beignets/pretzels despite its chain format, and **Hearth and Hill** or **'mina** for a full restaurant with a genuinely substantiated dessert program.
