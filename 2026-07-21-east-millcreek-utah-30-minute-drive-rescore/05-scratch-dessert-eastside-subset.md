# Scratch-dessert east-side subset — tight East Millcreek corridor

Compiled 2026-07-16 from all Phase 4/5 records classified or researched so far, using `05-scratch-dessert-corridor-classified.md` as a reconciliation baseline but rechecking the wider classified candidate universe for dessert-specific production evidence. No new restaurants were discovered.

## Geographic rule

Included: Millcreek, East Millcreek/Foothill, Sugar House, 9th & 9th, 15th & 15th, and immediately touching parts of Liberty Wells and Central City that a local would reasonably treat as the same east-side outing. This includes the 500–700 East edge around 9th/13th South, but not downtown or Central Ninth. No classified Harvard/Yale or Avenues venue met the scratch-dessert evidence threshold.

“Open after 8” means posted service continues later than 8 PM; an 8 PM close is a **boundary**. Hours are the current operating evidence already supported in this run, not a guarantee against same-day changes.
