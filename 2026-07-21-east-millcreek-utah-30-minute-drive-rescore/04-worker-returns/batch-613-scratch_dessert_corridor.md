## Virg's Fish & Chips

- **Identity:** Current directory/review surfaces identify `Virg's`, `5770 S Redwood Rd, Taylorsville, UT 84123`, `(801) 968-7180`, and `https://virgsfamilyrestaurant.com/`. The submitted discovery record is OSM way 1047680010. NetWaiter displays ZIP `84129`, conflicting with the candidate and other current sources' `84123`. A 2020 owner profile identifies proprietor Dave Law and explains that the name came from Virginia, called `Virg`, the prior owner's wife.
- **Identity sources:** [official site](https://virgsfamilyrestaurant.com/), first-party but with little extractable text; [Wanderlog](https://wanderlog.com/place/details/2788422/virgs), [Restaurantji](https://www.restaurantji.com/ut/salt-lake-city/virgs-/), [Restaurant Guru](https://restaurantguru.com/Virgs-Taylorsville), and NetWaiter, directory/review surfaces; [Cyprus Credit Union owner profile](https://learn.cypruscu.com/a-delicious-tale-of-four-cities-virgs-restaurant?hs_amp=true), dated 2020; all accessed 2026-07-15. The Cyprus profile says the business started in 1981 and changed hands in 1990, while a 2016 Tooele Transcript Bulletin history says 1963 and 1991; the conflict is preserved.

- **Menu quotations:** Restaurant Guru's owner-uploaded menu includes `Our Specialties`, breakfasts and eggs, `The Cheepies`, breakfast sandwiches, griddle dishes, Benedicts, omelettes/scrambles/skillets, burgers, `Fish & Chips & More`, smothered burritos, hot and cold sandwiches, Mexican combination plates, `Smaller Appetite`, dinners and beverages. Fish offerings include two- through four-piece cod, fantail shrimp and chicken strips; other items include Loco Moco, pork chops and eggs, pancakes, French toast, burgers, meatloaf, steak, tacos, enchiladas, chimichangas and fruit plates.

It also names grilled onions and fried chicken. A dated 2008 Salt Lake Tribune comparison at this exact location described cod or halibut as `Fresh and moist on the inside` and the fish as `crunchy` and `greaseless`; this is a historical critic observation, not a current operator production statement. No current first-party claim about fish battering, cutting, thawing, sourcing or house-made components was retrieved.
- **Seasonality/turnover quotations:** The owner-uploaded menu describes the fruit plate and small fruit plate with `seasonal fruit`. No rotating-specials cadence, seasonal full-menu schedule, limited-run item calendar or sold-out/turnover statement was retrieved.

The 2008 critic report names cod or halibut but cannot establish current procurement. No farm, fishery, butcher, produce grower, bread supplier, named local supplier, geographic provenance, sustainability claim or current fresh-fish claim was retrieved.
- **Review-text quotations — food/product:** A Google customer report reproduced by Restaurant Guru calls the restaurant comfort food and locally owned. Another reproduced customer says their family had visited for more than ten years and had not received a bad, cold, lukewarm, undercooked or overcooked meal. Wanderlog reproduces customer reports of an omelet prepared as ordered, large portions, and pork chops with eggs. The 2008 Salt Lake Tribune critic praised the fish's moist interior and crunchy, greaseless exterior but called the fries `lukewarm and over-fried`.
- **Review-text quotations — service, drinks, price, parking, ambiance:** NetWaiter displays takeout and delivery, no catering, no reservations, no alcohol, no outdoor seating and private parking. A Restaurantji February 2023 customer reports an unfriendly reception, a long wait and staff complaints about customers. An August 2022 customer reports a waiter ignored one diner, rolled his eyes and was sarcastic. These are attributed customer reports, not operator admissions.
- **Potentially adverse factual quotations:** The 2020 owner profile says Virg's operated four locations—Erda, Taylorsville, Ogden and West Valley—and had expanded from its fish-and-chips/chicken-strips origin to old-fashioned diner breakfast and lunch. The current menu spans breakfast, burgers, fish and chips, Mexican plates, sandwiches, steak, meatloaf and commercial Coke and Pepsi products. The 2008 critic reported lukewarm, over-fried fries, and recent customers report service problems. No Virg's-specific statement of frozen fish, premade food, microwave reheating, boil-in-bag components or commissary production was retrieved.
- **Cuisine/format:** Current and historical sources describe a family restaurant / old-fashioned diner centered historically on fish and chips and chicken strips, with breakfast, American diner food, burgers, sandwiches, Mexican plates and dinners. Current directory sources expose dine-in, takeout and delivery, private parking, no reservations and no alcohol. The 2020 profile documents a locally owned multi-location operation at that time.
