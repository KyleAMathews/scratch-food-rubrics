## Taste of Punjab

- **Identity:** Historical sources resolve the coordinate to `Taste of Punjab`, 1241 E 8600 S, Sandy, UT 84094, `(801) 256-3500`. No surviving Utah official domain was verified. Current Utah DABS records identify `Bhutan House Restaurant`, `(801) 875-7640`, at the same address, establishing a successor occupant; Taste of Punjab evidence is historical.
- **Identity sources:** [LatLong/OSM coordinate record](https://www.latlong.net/poi/taste-of-punjab-336772), coordinate source; [Allmenus](https://www.allmenus.com/ut/sandy/38751-taste-of-punjab/menu/), archived menu/directory; [MerchantCircle](https://www.merchantcircle.com/taste-of-punjab-sandy-ut), historical review directory; [Utah DABS current license list](https://abs.utah.gov/wp-content/uploads/licensee_list.pdf), government/current occupant; all accessed 2026-07-15.
- **Rating evidence:** MerchantCircle displays `4.4` from `18 reviews`. No current direct Google, Yelp, Tripadvisor or Facebook rating for the Sandy operation was retrieved. These reviews are historical, mainly dated 2008–2011.
- **Price evidence:** Allmenus displays appetizers `$2.25–7.99`, breads `$1.99–3.99`, mains about `$11.50–18.99`, desserts `$3.50–3.95`, and beverages `$1.95–3.75`; it labels the restaurant `$$$$$`. Yellow Pages historically labels `$$`. No current prices exist for the closed/replaced identity.
- **Hours/day-part evidence:** MerchantCircle historically displays Monday–Saturday `11:05 am–10:00 pm` and Sunday `4:00 pm–8:00 pm`; reviews mention a lunch buffet and dinner. These are not current hours. Current DABS identifies Bhutan House at the address, and no current Taste of Punjab service window was found.
- **Menu quotations:** Archived menu categories include appetizers, soups/salads, tandoori breads, chicken, lamb, vegetables, biryani, seafood, tandoori specialties, desserts and beverages. Items span samosas/pakoras, naan/parantha/roti, tikka masala, korma, vindaloo, saag, curry, karahi, biryani, mahi-mahi, tandoori mixed grill, mango ice cream, gulab jamun, kheer and lassi.
