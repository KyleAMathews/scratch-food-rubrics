- **Identity:** University sources display **“Crimson View”** or **“Crimson View @ A. Ray Olpin Student Union,”** fourth floor / Level 4, **A. Ray Olpin Student Union, 200 Central Campus Drive, Salt Lake City, UT 84112**. Current Restaurantji displays phone **(801) 581-7257**; Union room reservations use **801-581-7251**; Allmenus' historical restaurant record displays **(801) 581-8191**. Official institutional domains include `https://www.union.utah.edu/` and University of Utah Dining/Housing pages.
