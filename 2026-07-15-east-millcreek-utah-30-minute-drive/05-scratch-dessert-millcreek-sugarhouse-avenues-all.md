# All confirmed scratch-dessert places — Millcreek, Sugar House, and Avenues corridor

Completeness audit compiled 2026-07-16 from all completed Phase 4 returns, the two earlier east-side dessert summaries, repair artifacts, and the worker index. Scope is Millcreek/East Millcreek, Sugar House, 9th & 9th, 15th & 15th, the Avenues, and immediately adjacent east-central neighborhoods. This is an evidence inventory, not a ranking or score.

For the hours table, **yes** means the business is posted open *after* 8 PM on that weekday. **Boundary** means it closes at 8 PM and is not a dependable 8 PM arrival. Hours were rechecked against current operator pages where available on 2026-07-16; otherwise the cited Phase 4 return is the source.

## Result

- **16 confirmed scratch-dessert producers:** 11 dessert-forward businesses and 5 restaurants with a meaningful scratch-dessert program.
- **8 are clearly open after 8 PM every weekday:** Parfé Diem, Matcha Cafe Kyoto, Greenhouse Effect, Thirst, Franco's Churro House, Hearth and Hill, 'mina, and Salt & Olive.
- **Rawtopia is an additional clear Friday option.** Picnic Cafe is a conditional Friday option because evening service is posted but dessert-counter availability was not separately proven.
- The prior 15-place classified file omitted **R-0457 Franco's Churro House**, whose completed supplemental return is now indexed. The earlier 12-place corridor inventory also omitted Banbury Cross, Tulie, Greenhouse Effect, and Thirst.

## Duplicate and identity reconciliation

- **Tulie's Cafe / Tulie Bakery (R-2504)** is one same-address entity. The former is the OSM/index alias; the latter is the current official identity.
- **Table X Bread** and the nighttime **Table X restaurant** share a brand/address, but only the bakery's scratch-pastry evidence is counted here; restaurant hours do not make the bakery a late dessert option.
- **Hill's Kitchen (R-1905)** and **Hearth and Hill (R-1859)** share 2188 S Highland Dr and an ownership group, but they are distinct operating concepts with distinct dessert evidence. Neither is collapsed into the other.
- **Banbury Cross (R-2290)** had an address mismatch in the candidate record; the official current address is 705 S 700 E.
- No duplicate rows remain in the 16-place count.
