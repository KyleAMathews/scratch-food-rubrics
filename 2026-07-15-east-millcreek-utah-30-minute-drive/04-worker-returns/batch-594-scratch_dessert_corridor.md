## Log Haven

- **Identity:** Official site displays `Log Haven`, `6451 E Millcreek Canyon Rd`, `Salt Lake City, Utah 84109`, `(801)272-8255`, and `https://log-haven.com/`. No alias or local-script name was displayed. Current culinary page names Todd Hoffee as chef, Dayton Case and Steve Takahashi as sous chefs, Takahashi also as pastry chef, and Dave Jones as culinary advisor.

- **Menu quotations:** Current official headings are `Small Plates`, `Greens`, `Entrées`, `Sides`, and `Sweets`. Small plates include house-chip Alpine nachos, Piedmontese bresaola, miso crab cakes, Korean fried cauliflower, tuna tartare, and grilled New Zealand calamari. Entrées include honey-balsamic chicken, Lan-Rock pork chop, bison striploin, salmon, filet mignon, ricotta gnocchi, market vegetables, elk loin, market steak au poivre, and ahi tuna tataki, with preparations spanning grits, polenta, congee, yakisoba, demi-glace, gastrique, pesto, reductions, and aiolis. Sweets include frozen coconut-banana soufflé, maple crème brûlée, daily ice creams/sorbets, Liège waffle, brioche bread pudding, and ganache tart.
- **Production/process quotations:** Current official menu says `DAILY HOUSE MADE ICE CREAMS AND SORBETS`, `house chocolate ice cream`, `house chips`, `house black truffle sea salt butter`, and `house spice blend stir fry sauce`. Menu techniques/physical forms include shrimp-nori mousseline, miso-sesame tuile, pickled shiitake/avocado/blueberries/onions, smoked pepper chimichurri, brown-butter grits, stewed apricots, pan-seared salmon, ricotta gnocchi, charred vegetables, duck-fat potatoes, and `fermeted black bean-yakitori`. In a dated 2015 interview, then chef/owner Dave Jones said he foraged and used native plants and cultivated plants from the Log Haven ecosystem, `from which I make sauces, shrubs for specialty cocktails, sorbets, ice creams and other delicacies.`
- **Seasonality/turnover quotations:** Salt Lake Magazine wrote in May 2024 that `the menu changes seasonally` and named then-recent seasonal crab cakes, elk striploin, and dill-pressed salmon. Current official menu uses `MARKET VEGETABLES` and `MARKET STEAK AU POIVRE`; the official private-event page says menus use `seasonal ingredients`. Official pages also expose Thanksgiving and dated special-event menus. No exact recurring calendar for full-menu changes was retrieved.
- **Ingredient/sourcing quotations:** Current first-party page states, `Log Haven has always purchased local/regional organic and natural ingredients whenever possible`, supports local producers focused on sustainability/conservation, and says Chef Hoffee partners with local/regional purveyors and provides `fresh organic produce from his own garden`; it also states a commitment to selling food close to where grown. Current menu names forest mushrooms, Piedmontese bresaola, New Zealand calamari, Lan-Rock pork, bison, elk, Drunken goat cheese, Morita chile, wild huckleberry, and ruby royal apricots. The dated 2015 interview states foraging/cultivating native ecosystem plants. No individual farm or purveyor names were retrieved.
- **Review-text quotations — food/product:** A December 2025 Tripadvisor customer reports thick batter on guinea fowl, smoky/dry prawns, dry/tasteless gnocchi that seemed to have sat under a heat lamp, and a tasteless steak, while calling the ice cream good. A January 2026 customer calls dessert and appetizers good but steak comparable to less expensive restaurants. A 2024 customer reports quail, elk, tenderloin, and warm chocolate cake with coffee ice cream as delicious. A Restaurantji customer says food was well prepared and presentation excellent. These are attributed customer reports.
- **Review-text quotations — service, drinks, price, parking, ambiance:** Recent Tripadvisor customers praise the canyon/log-cabin setting and attentive staff but report slow service, an abrupt front-desk employee, bread sold separately, and valet-only parking unless limited roadside spaces are available. A May 2025 customer says the server checked less often after the restaurant became busy and says parking is valet apart from limited street spaces. OpenTable lists valet parking and live music Friday–Sunday `6:30 - 9:30 p.m.`

Recent Tripadvisor customers provide the preparation/service/price complaints quoted above. Official/current platform service hours conflict, and chef attribution differs between the official current team page (Todd Hoffee, Dave Jones culinary advisor) and OpenTable (Dave Jones executive chef).
