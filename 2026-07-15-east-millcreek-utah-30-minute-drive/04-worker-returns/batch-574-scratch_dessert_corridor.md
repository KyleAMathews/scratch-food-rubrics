- **Review-text quotations — food/product:** A June 2025 Google review reproduced by Wanderlog calls the amuse-bouche, beef tartare, seafood, espresso, and first bite of pork delicious, but says the Bolognese was `crumbled ground beef atop pasta`, pork became `increasingly drier`, the potato rosette was `dried up`, and tiramisù was dry because `the lady fingers were not soaked long enough in the espresso`; the reviewer says its cocoa appearance suggested it `was not made that day` ([Wanderlog](https://wanderlog.com/place/details/1154171/veneto-ristorante-italiano)). A May 2025 reviewer says a palate-cleanser course of two rum-soaked citrus slices did not `quite` stand up as a full course, and that a returning diner preferred courses from previous seasons. Visit Salt Lake says pear/pecorino fillings complemented one another and the pasta felt light ([Visit Salt Lake](https://www.visitsaltlake.com/articles/post/we-ate-at-the-best-restaurants-in-salt-lake-city/)).
