- **Operator/history quotations:** A 2019 Voices of Utah feature calls Juanita a **“small family-owned business”** specializing in classic Salvadorian food. It identifies Carolina Vides as the owners' daughter, says she was born in Cabañas, Sensuntepeque, and reports that the family moved to the United States in the 1990s. The family had previously run a restaurant in El Salvador and was **“cooking and making the same pupusas that they love”** there; the Salt Lake restaurant had opened about four years before publication.
