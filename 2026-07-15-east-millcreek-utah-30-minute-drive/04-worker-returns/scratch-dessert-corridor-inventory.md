# Scratch-dessert corridor inventory — classified so far

Inventory date: 2026-07-16. Evidence access date remains the Phase 4 frozen date, 2026-07-15.

Scope: completed Phase 4 returns only, for Millcreek/East Millcreek, Sugar House, 9th & 9th, 15th & 15th, and the immediately adjacent east-central Salt Lake neighborhoods. This is an evidence inventory, not a score, ranking, or Phase 5 classification. “Open at 8 p.m.” means only that an existing worker return explicitly gives hours encompassing 8 p.m.; it is not a new real-time hours check.

## A. Dedicated dessert, bakery, or cafe destinations

### R-2008 — Parfé Diem

- **Area/address:** Sugar House, 2040 S 1000 E, Salt Lake City, UT 84105.
- **Exact scratch evidence:** Official menu says its parfaits include “homemade chocolate and vanilla wafers, made from scratch.” Apple Maps' business description says the shop bakes vanilla and chocolate wafers “entirely from scratch”; SLUG calls them “house-made wafers.” Existing evidence also records ovens/baking and a self-taught baker-founder.
- **Sources:** [official home/menu](https://parfediem.com/), [official about](https://parfediem.com/about), [Apple Maps](https://maps.apple.com/place?place-id=ID896CBE5F8C8B50C), [SLUG](https://www.slugmag.com/community/food/food-reviews/salt-lake-citys-finest-pop-ups/).
- **Confidence:** High. First-party item-specific scratch wording, supported by independent descriptions.
- **8 p.m.:** Yes. Existing official evidence says daily 11 a.m.–11 p.m.

- **8 p.m.:** Yes. Existing official/platform evidence says Sun–Thu 11 a.m.–9 p.m. and Fri–Sat 11 a.m.–10 p.m.

### R-1762 — Sidecar Doughnuts & Coffee

- **Area/address:** Sugar House, 701 E 2100 S, Salt Lake City, UT 84106.
- **Exact scratch evidence:** Official location says “made-from-scratch, fresh-hourly doughnuts … all made in-house, every day.” Official About describes mixing/rolling dough, simmering fruit purees, cooking bacon, whipping creams/custards, and frying small batches all day. Founder says, “Every glaze, every topping, every filling, everything’s in house.” Reporter observed dough being cut, fried, and glazed.
- **Sources:** [official location](https://sidecardoughnuts.com/location/salt-lake-city/), [official About](https://sidecardoughnuts.com/about/), [official menu](https://sidecardoughnuts.com/menu/), [Deseret News profile](https://www.deseret.com/lifestyle/2026/01/23/sidecar-doughnuts-opens-in-salt-lake-city/).
- **Confidence:** High. Multiple first-party process claims plus reporter observation. It is a multi-location chain, but the evidence specifically substantiates local in-house production.
- **8 p.m.:** No. The latest existing hours evidence closes no later than 7 p.m., with conflicting earlier closing times.

Official menu labels vanilla, Earl Grey, and almond syrups “House Made Syrups” and lists daily rotating fruit danish/food specials.

- **Confidence:** High. Named owner-baker and explicit fresh-daily pastry production.
- **8 p.m.:** No. Existing official evidence says Wed–Sun 8 a.m.–2 p.m.

## Strict exclusions from this inventory

These nearby completed candidates looked dessert-relevant but did not meet the requested substantiation threshold:

- **R-1305 Beaumont Bakery & Cafe:** strong explicit in-house bread, sauce, turkey, and sourdough evidence, plus a substantial pastry menu, but the retrieved return never explicitly says its pastries/desserts are produced in-house. Category/name alone was not promoted into scratch-dessert proof.
- **R-1227 Kahve Cafe:** strong small-kitchen cooking, handmade catering, house-made tahini, seasonal rotation, and baked-goods menu evidence, but the return explicitly stops short of proving its breads/baklava/cakes are made on site.
- **R-1966 Happy Camper Deli & Bakery:** only Restaurantji summary wording says bakery items are homemade/made in-house; first-party evidence proves baked rustic sourdough/sourdough crust but not dessert production. This was treated as insufficient under the prompt's strict rule.
- **R-0887 Coffee Garden:** pastries, cake, bread pudding, and daily/seasonal items are documented, but the return says pastry/quiche/cake/bread-pudding production is unavailable.
- **R-1559 Violet:** official generic “made from scratch” wording and a pastry case are documented, but detailed pastry production is explicitly unavailable; the evidence does not safely connect the generic claim to the desserts.
- **R-1965 Perch Coffee:** its pastries are explicitly sourced from 43 Bakery and cinnamon rolls from Dough Lady, so it is not a scratch-dessert producer itself.
