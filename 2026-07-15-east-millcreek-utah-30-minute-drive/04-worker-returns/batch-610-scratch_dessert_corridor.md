## Taqueria 27 Lehi

- **Identity:** Official location page displays `Taqueria 27`, `1688 W Traverse Pkwy, Lehi, UT 84043`, `801-331-8033`, and `https://taqueria27.com/`. Lehi's business list displays `TAQUERIA 27 LEHI LLC` and the same address/phone. No local-script name was displayed.
- **Identity sources:** [official locations](https://taqueria27.com/taqueria-27-locations), [official About/home](https://taqueria27.com/), [official food menu](https://taqueria27.com/-food-menu), all first-party; [Lehi business-license list](https://www.lehi-ut.gov/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/Full-Lehi-Business-License-List-as-of-June-2024.pdf), municipal source; [Utah alcohol-license list](https://abs.utah.gov/wp-content/uploads/licensee_list.pdf), government source; [Restaurantji](https://www.restaurantji.com/ut/lehi/taqueria-27-lehi-/), direct review/directory platform; all accessed 2026-07-15.
- **Rating evidence:** Restaurantji displays `4.5`, `610 ratings`, and response shares `5 80%`, `4 8%`, `3 3%`, `2 3%`, `1 6%`. Tripadvisor displays `4.1 (18 reviews)` and `#20 of 138 Restaurants in Lehi`. Restaurant Guru displays `Google (4.4/5) 1949`, `Trip (4.1/5) 18`, `Facebook (5/5) 2`, `Opentable (4/5) 8`, and Foursquare `Not rated yet`. Wanderlog displays Google `4.4 (1961)` and Tripadvisor `4.1 (18)`.
- **Price evidence:** Official current menu displays guacamole sampler and various items with prices including tacos about `$9.99–$13.99`, sides `$4.00`, burrito/bowl or smothering upgrades `$4.50`, draft pint `$6.99` and pitcher `$14.99`, house margarita mix `$13.99/32 oz`, and hot-sauce bottles `$7.49`. Tripadvisor displays `$$ - $$$`; Restaurant Guru customer reports include `$10–20`; OpenTable displays `Up to $30` on localized surfaces.
- **Hours/day-part evidence:** Official Lehi location displays Sunday–Thursday `11:30 am to 9 pm` and Friday–Saturday `11:30 am to 10 pm`. Restaurantji displays 11AM–9PM Sunday–Thursday and 11AM–10PM Friday–Saturday. Wanderlog displays 11AM–8PM Sunday–Thursday and 11AM–9PM Friday–Saturday. Sources expose lunch and dinner; the current official menu says its separate brunch section is only at Eagle Mountain and Downtown, not Lehi.
- **Menu quotations:** Official menu presents guacamole/salsa/chips, tacos, enchiladas, fajitas, taquitos, burritos/bowls, salads, sides, beer, tequila/mezcal, cocktails, margaritas, hot sauce, and location-limited brunch. Taco fillings include veggie, bacon-wrapped jalapeño/carne asada, buffalo fried chicken, grilled/blackened salmon or shrimp, turkey chorizo, citrus carnitas, pork belly, pork chile verde, grilled chicken, carne asada, and fish; accompaniments include slaw, cremas/aiolis, pico, pickled onion, queso fresco, rice/beans, and chimichurri.
- **Production/process quotations:** Official About says, `Our food is made from scratch`, and that each location has its own culinary team. Current menu labels `House Made Turkey Chorizo`, `house-made pear simple syrup`, `fresh cooked corn tortilla chips`, and T27 corn/flour tortillas; it describes slow-cooked shredded salsa chicken, crispy pork belly, grilled/blackened seafood, duck confit, pickled onion, fried leeks, fresh-muddled fruit/mint, and house margarita mix. A chain ordering page says pork belly is `house smoked, sliced and seared`; this item was retrieved from the Downtown page rather than the Lehi ordering page.
- **Seasonality/turnover quotations:** Official About states each location's culinary team creates `unique specials that change daily`, with taco, guacamole, cocktail, fish, and dessert of the day displayed on a specials board. Official menu lists a `Seasonal Draft`. OpenTable reviewers in 2026 mention trying specials. No fixed seasonal full-menu-change calendar was retrieved.
- **Ingredient/sourcing quotations:** Official About says the group partners with suppliers for `the best ingredients available. Local when possible, sustainable, and always fresh.` Current menu specifically describes `Fresh Fish Of The Day` as `fresh and sustainably sourced fish`. Ingredients include duck confit, pork belly, turkey chorizo, salmon, shrimp, carne asada, Oaxaca/queso fresco/gorgonzola, squash, spinach, corn, garbanzo, pumpkin seeds, avocado, and named beer/spirit brands. No individual farm, fishery, butcher, tortilla producer, or supplier was retrieved.
- **Review-text quotations — food/product:** A recent Google customer reproduced by Restaurant Guru says two small tacos cost over $12 and could not be mixed, while another praises shrimp tostada bites and a strawberry cake as not too sweet. An OpenTable January 2026 customer reports cold food that seemed like leftovers. Wanderlog reproduces praise for fried-chicken tacos, gluten-free chips/churros, shrimp enchiladas, fajitas and salsa, plus a customer calling the food bland. These are attributed customer reports.
- **Review-text quotations — service, drinks, price, parking, ambiance:** Restaurantji lists outdoor seating, private dining, cocktails, takeout and delivery. Wanderlog customers praise several named servers and atmosphere. The adverse OpenTable customer reports being forgotten by the server and paying almost `$180`; other 2026 OpenTable reviewers praise food, service, and specials.
- **Potentially adverse factual quotations:** Official About/location page displays five Utah locations, and Tripadvisor says the group employs more than 100 people across them. Menu includes branded packaged/draft beer and spirits alongside location culinary production. Official menu warns it cannot guarantee against cross-contamination. The adverse OpenTable customer reports cold food perceived as leftovers; this is a customer observation, not an operator statement. No commissary, boil-in-bag, microwave, or frozen-production wording was retrieved.
- **Cuisine/format:** Official sources describe a locally and veteran-owned multi-location taqueria/tequila bar with globally influenced Mexican food, daily specials, large tequila/mezcal selection, reservations, catering, private events, online ordering, and location-level culinary teams. Tripadvisor labels Mexican and American; Restaurantji emphasizes tacos, guacamole, salsa, and enchiladas. Lehi offers lunch/dinner, bar, patio, delivery/takeout, and private dining.
- **Neutral factual claims:** First-party sources support identity, current locations/hours/menu/prices, general production statement, specifically named house/fresh-cooked/smoked components, daily location-level specials, supplier policy, sustainably sourced fish wording, and multi-location operation. Government sources support the current Lehi LLC/license identity. Reviews support only their attributed physical, service, price, and ambiance observations. Downtown-specific pork-belly wording was not presented as confirmed Lehi preparation.
